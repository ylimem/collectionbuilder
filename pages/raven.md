## Raven's Wildest Story

Once upon a time, Raven went to New Haven, Connecticut, for a conference at Yale University. On their way back to visit a friend in NYC, however, they encountered a very interesting man at the Amtrak train who only spoke Spanish and wanted someone to accompany him on a train to New York City.
